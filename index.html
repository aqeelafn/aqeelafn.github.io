About Me

A brief introduction

I'm a fresh graduate from Telkom University with a degree in Data Science, graduating with Cum Laude honors (GPA 3.81/4.00). My journey in data science has been enriched by hands-on experience as a Data Analyst Intern at PT Pelita Air Service and Telkom University, where I transformed raw data into actionable insights through interactive dashboards and comprehensive analytics.

As a Teaching Assistant for 4 semesters, I've guided over 100+ students through complex technical concepts in AI, Operating Systems, and various foundational computing courses. This experience has sharpened my ability to communicate complex ideas clearly and work collaboratively in diverse teams.

Beyond technical skills, I'm deeply passionate about leveraging data to drive meaningful change. I've published 2 international papers on machine learning applications in healthcare and received the Best Presenter Award at an international conference. I'm actively seeking opportunities in data analytics, business intelligence, and management trainee programs where data plays a strategic role.
